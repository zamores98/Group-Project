Our History

We are an anime streaming and merchandising business. We were founded at Virginia Commonwealth university. Our brand is headed by Phuc Nguyen and Zach Amores.
We have always had dreams of building and managing an anime website/business, we finally decided to make our brand in 2018. Our favorite anime is One Piece.
Our goal is to satisfy anime fans all around the world and to share their love for anime on our website with monthly promotions.
